Interactive EN→HI translation demo with two model modes: a fast distilled LSTM and a more accurate Transformer trained from scratch on OPUS-100.

## What it does

- Real-time translation in the browser
- Switch between speed-optimized and accuracy-optimized models
- Production-style training pipeline with RunPod deployment

## Stack

PyTorch · custom `transformer_lib` · OPUS-100 · ONNX/serving
