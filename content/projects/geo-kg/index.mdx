Builds a **geometry knowledge graph** from olympiad PDFs: problems, theorems, concepts, motifs, and proof tactics — with visualization and theorem recommendation.

## Progress

- PDF ingestion via Docling, problem segmentation, entity extraction
- Ontology growth and graph build (`geo-kg` CLI)
- Processed *Narrative Approaches to International Mathematical Problems*
- HTML/PNG graph visualization

## Stack

Docling · PyMuPDF · YAML ontology · NetworkX · Matplotlib
